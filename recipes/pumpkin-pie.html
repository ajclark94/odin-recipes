Chef John's Pumkin Pie

Description

This is Chef John's rendition of pumpkin pie that I found on All Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumkin puree
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon Chinese 5-spice powder
  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F (220 C)
  2. Whisk together pumpkin, egg yolks, and egg in large bowl until smooth. Add sweetened condensed milk, cinnamon, ginger, salt, nutmeg, and Chinese 5-spice powder; whisk until thoroughly combined
  3. Line a 9-inch pie plate with pie crust; crimp edges
  4. Pour filling into crust; lightly tap on the counter to release air bubbles
  5. Bake for 15 minutes
  6. Reduce heat to 350 F (175 C) and bake until just set in the middle, 30 to 40 more minutes. A paring knife inserted into the filling, 1 inch from the edge, should come out clean. Allow to cool completely before serving
